YouTube Se Paise Kaise Kamaye 💰

YouTube ek powerful platform hai jahan se aap ghar baithe earning kar sakte ho. Earning aapke content, niche, audience aur consistency par depend karti hai.

🎯 1. Basic Earning (Ads)

YouTube AdSense ke through creators ko paise deta hai. Income RPM aur audience location par depend karti hai.

  • 🔹 1000 views = ₹20 – ₹150
  • 🔹 1 lakh views = ₹2,000 – ₹15,000
  • 🔹 10 lakh views = ₹20,000 – ₹1,50,000+

🔥 2. Example

Agar aapki 10 videos par 1 lakh views aate hain, to earning ₹20,000 se ₹1.5 lakh ya usse bhi zyada ho sakti hai.

⚠️ Monetization Requirement

YouTube Partner Program ke liye generally:

  • ✅ 1000 Subscribers
  • ✅ 4000 Watch Hours

💡 Reality Check

  • 👉 Start: ₹0 – ₹1000
  • 👉 3–6 Months: ₹5K – ₹50K
  • 👉 Consistency ke saath ₹1 Lakh+ possible
